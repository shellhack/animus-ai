REGARDING THE POKÉMON BLUE ROM
==============================

This repository does not include a Pokémon Blue ROM file.

Pokémon Blue (Game Boy, 1996) is copyrighted software owned by Nintendo and Game Freak.
Distributing ROM files without authorization is a violation of copyright law.

To run ANIMUS, you must provide your own legally obtained copy of the ROM.
The expected filename is: pokemon_azul.gb (or rename your file accordingly)

ANIMUS is environment-agnostic in principle. Pokémon Blue was chosen as a test
environment because of its well-documented RAM structure and complex multi-map geography.
The agent has no knowledge of game mechanics, characters, or narrative — it perceives
only map ID and (x, y) coordinates.

Future versions may support open-source or custom environments that do not
require third-party software.
